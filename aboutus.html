About
Our visionary Pushpraj sharma, (along with his small team) being a food lover himself cameup with this self articulated idea of bringing the urban culture and ease of lifestyle here in our city Pipariya.
So we've Designed this website for the tech savy smartphone generation who loves both, the food from different corners of this town and the ease of their home.
Our technology platform connects customers & restaurant partners serving their multiple needs. Customers use our platform to search and discover restaurants. On the other hand, we provide restaurant partners with industry-specific marketing tools which enable them to engage and acquire customers to grow their business while also providing a reliable and efficient last mile delivery service.
Soon we are planning to expand and include every small food joint to bring them directly to your work desks at home with your support.
